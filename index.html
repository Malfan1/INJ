Step-by-step guide

  1. Head over to Ninja.Garden and click on "Join The Garden"
  2. Sign in via X (Twitter).

Neptune Finance is a collateral-based lending and borrowing platform built using Rust and the Cosmos SDK, a development kit designed for interoperable networks.

Step-by-step guide

  1. Connect your wallet to the Injective testnet and copy your wallet address.
  2. Use your copied wallet address to receive tokens from the Injective faucet.
  3. Now with tokens in your wallet head to inj-testnet.nept.finance to connect to the application.
  4. To lend to the market, simply open the lending module by clicking the “LEND” button for your token and enter the amount you would like to deposit. When the deposit is complete, you will receive nTokens as receipt tokens used to redeem the deposit plus accrued interest.
  5. Optionally, you can choose "Lend and Collateralize" during the lending process. This combines lending and using the deposit as collateral for a borrow subaccount. Using a receipt token as collateral allows you to earn a lending yield while retaining the ability to borrow against it.
  6. Go to the Borrow page to view information about your accounts and tokens. To create a subaccount, click the account icon. Provide collateral to open a subaccount. Accepted collateral tokens for testnet include: INJ, USDC, and USDT, along with their receipt token variants (nINJ, nUSDC, nUSDT).
